About David Maus Jr.

My Story

A couple of years ago, I walked away from a 20-year career in the car business. I was a vice president overseeing more than 400 employees. On paper, it was everything I had worked for.

But deep down, I knew God was calling me to something completely different.

It didn't make sense.
And when I finally said yes, it cost me.

It fractured my relationship with my dad.
Close friends chose sides.
Some never spoke to me again.

No calls. No support. Just silence.

All I had was my wife, my kids, and God.
And honestly, that was enough.

Not long after, I posted a simple, low-budget video of myself building a DIY cold plunge. I wasn't trying to start a business. I wasn't thinking about content or income.

I was just trying to feel alive again.

That video became a spark.

People from all over the world started reaching out, sharing their stories, their struggles, and how something as simple as cold water was changing their lives. And something in me said, keep going.

So I did.

Fast forward to today, and by God's grace, that one step of obedience turned into a thriving YouTube channel focused on health and wellness.

I'm not a doctor.
I didn't grow up an athlete.
I'm not anything special.

I'm just a guy who got curious, searched for answers, and decided to share what I was learning.

And somehow, every single month, over a million dollars in products are sold through my content.

That still doesn't feel real.

But the impact — that's what matters.

People are being helped.
Encouraged.
Inspired to take control of their health and believe that change is possible.

None of this came from a perfect strategy.

There was no 10-step plan.
No guru.
No course.

Every day, I simply said, "God, this is Yours. Use it however You want."

Whether a video reached one person or thousands, I kept showing up. And God kept providing step by step, in ways I never could have planned.

Today, I've been able to travel with my family, be present with my kids, and live a life I didn't even know was possible.

That's all I ever wanted.

Not the houses.
Not the cars.
Just time.

If you're reading this and feel stuck, tired, confused, or living a life that looks good on the outside but feels empty inside… I get it.

I've been there.

Let me tell you this:

You can reinvent your life.

It won't happen overnight.
You don't need a perfect plan.

You need a surrendered heart, consistent effort, and the courage to stop letting other people define your limits.

Because you don't have one.

God has a plan bigger than anything you could map out. You may not be able to see it yet, but you can trust Him.

If everything ended tomorrow, I'd still be grateful.

Because these have been the richest years of my life — not in money, but in meaning, growth, and purpose.

To everyone who has supported the journey, thank you.
To my wife and kids, you're my anchor.
And to God, I give it all back. Every bit of it.

Don't settle for a life that looks good but feels off.
Take the step. Do the work. Trust God with the rest.

I'm David Maus Jr.
And this is just the beginning.
